BEST DECISION I MADE FOR SCHENGEN VISA /Elena Chereshneva
After weeks of trying to find an appointment at the Italian consulate, I finally got one for September. I wanted to travel to Europe for the summer, but it seemed impossible because all the consulates were fully booked. That's when I saw an ad for Visa Penguin. I didn't expect much, but the very next day after signing up, Visa Penguin notified me about open slots at the French embassy for the following day! I quickly booked the appointment, and I couldn't believe it. After weeks of searching, Visa Penguin was the answer I needed. I highly recommend Visa Penguin to anyone who is having trouble getting a visa appointment. The experience was great, and I want to thank Vlad and the Visabot team for their amazing service. If you're struggling to find an appointment, give Visa Penguin a try – you'll be glad you did!
